About this table: Aryabhatta divided the quarter circle (90°) into 24 equal parts of 3° 45′ each. The Devanagari syllables encode the cumulative sine differences using his unique āryabhaṭa numerical notation. R = 3438 arc-minutes. The Hindu-Arabic column shows the difference added at each step; Jyā (A) is the running cumulative total.
